Text-en: This diploma work tries to expose the context, the recent and fast history as well as the presence of French rap in the media. Then explain the difference between independent rap and commercial rap: when did the listeners begin to split the two and why? In order to carry out this research work, I articulated this exhibition around a course in five modules, which each time take up the major aspects developed in my work of memory. I borrowed from Stéphanie Molinero and her sociological inquiry. These parts are the artistic aspect, (the form) the moral aspect (the background) and the social aspect (the diffusion, the image) of rap music in France.
