Text-fr: Initialement imaginé comme un film, Auto-autopsie est le résultat évolutif d’un travail d'écriture de longue haleine, scénarisant des situations où s’entrechoquent objets et personnages allégoriques. Text-en: First imagined as a film, Auto-autopsy is the result of a lengthy writing job. Despite the distance it took little by little from its screenplay nature, the text still configures situations where allegoric objects and allegoric characters are interacting. The project is now taking possession of dancers. In order to adapt itself for its new scenic application, the text is now tearing and reordering itself into a scenographic and musical dispositive.
