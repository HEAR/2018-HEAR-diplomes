Text-en: Whit does not live with his time, one day he decides to go to ici, a place with blurred boundaries. He does not know what has come to ici and wants to understand it. To curb computer overpollution, Ambient-Al chose six children to grow up with a companion, an implanted artificial intelligence. Ucta was born earthly, but only lived in Léthé, the dematerialized city that stopped the demographic crisis. But Ucta is nostalgic for the Earth she did not know. Paris is in love with Jacob, he ends up integrating his research team as an astronaut for spatio-simulator. He learns why Jacob wants to leave Earth and decides to stop him. From these four news is born a research laboratory probing the transversality between editions and virtual worlds.
